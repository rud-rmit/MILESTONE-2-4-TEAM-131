How is this calculated?

Think of this like a balance scale:

1. We look at the "Normal" (The baseline of crashes for sunny/dry days).

2. We add the "Current Pressure" (How much do your chosen conditions, like 'Snowy' or 'Icy', normally increase the risk?).

3. If the "Pressure" weighs much heavier than "Normal," the Risk Score goes up!
